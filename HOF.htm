Our Story

From a simple passion for great fries to Tagoloan's favorite spot for flavorful meals

Where It All Began

House of Fries started with a simple belief: everyone deserves delicious, satisfying food without breaking the bank. In the heart of Tagoloan, we opened our doors with a mission to serve the biggest, crispiest, most flavorful fries in town.

What began as a small operation focused on perfecting the art of the perfect fry has grown into a beloved local spot known for generous portions, bold flavors, and affordable prices that keep our community coming back day after day.

Today, we're proud to serve not just fries, but a full menu of crowd-pleasing meals.
